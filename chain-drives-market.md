<p><p>Chain drives are types of mechanical power transmission systems used in various industries for transmitting power between two shafts. The transmission chain is the most commonly used type, featuring interlinked metal plates with teeth for efficient power transmission. Silent chain drives operate silently due to their toothed links with V-shaped grooves. Leaf chain is a robust type used mainly in forklifts and cranes. Roller chains consist of cylindrical rollers linked with plates, providing smooth and low-friction operation. The conveyor chain is designed for conveying bulk materials in industrial applications. Other types may include specialty chains for specific applications.</p></p>
