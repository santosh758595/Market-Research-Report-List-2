<p><p>The market for 5G radio frequency filters can be categorized into three types. Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters, which utilize the movement of sound waves on a piezoelectric substrate to filter signals. Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters, which use the vibration of a thin film on a substrate to filter signals. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various types of filters that may utilize different technologies such as ceramic or dielectric resonators. These filters play a crucial role in 5G technologies by removing unwanted signals and ensuring efficient communication.</p></p>
