<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive clutch material market comprises various types of clutches used in vehicles. Friction clutches utilize friction material to engage and disengage the engine from the transmission. Centrifugal clutches operate based on centrifugal force and engage when the engine reaches a certain speed. Semi-centrifugal clutches are similar, but allow for partial disengagement. Hydraulic clutches use hydraulic fluid to transmit power, while vacuum clutches rely on vacuum pressure. Electro-magnetic clutches utilize electromagnets to engage and disengage the clutch. Each type of clutch material has specific advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
