<p><p>Progressing cavity pumps, also known as PC pumps or positive displacement pumps, are widely used in various industries for handling viscous or abrasive fluids. These pumps operate using a rotating eccentric screw inside a stator, creating cavities that trap and transport fluids from the suction to the discharge side. They offer efficient and reliable pumping performance with low shear and gentle handling of fluids, making them suitable for applications in oil and gas, wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and chemical industries.</p><p>The progressing cavity pumps market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The market is driven by increasing demand for efficient and reliable pumping solutions in various industries. The rising adoption of PC pumps in the oil and gas sector, especially for handling highly viscous crude oil, is a major factor contributing to the market growth. Additionally, the growth of the wastewater treatment industry, especially in emerging economies, is further fueling the demand for PC pumps.</p><p>The market forecast indicates strong growth for the progressing cavity pumps market with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.7% during the forecasted period.
