<p><p>Cryogenic cables are specialized cables designed to operate in extremely low-temperature environments. There are two types available in the market - single-core cryogenic cables and multi-core cryogenic cables. Single-core cryogenic cables consist of a single conductor enclosed in an insulation material, ideal for applications requiring a single signal transmission. Multi-core cryogenic cables, on the other hand, have multiple conductors within a single cable, allowing for the transmission of multiple signals simultaneously. Both types cater to different needs in industries like healthcare, aerospace, and energy.</p></p>
