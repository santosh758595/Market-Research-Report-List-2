<p><p>Raman amplifiers are categorized into different types based on the wavelength range they operate in. C-Band Raman Amplifiers amplify signals in the C-Band wavelength range (1530-1565 nm), which is commonly used in optical communication systems. L-Band Raman Amplifiers operate in the L-Band wavelength range (1565-1625 nm), offering extended coverage beyond C-Band. C+L Band Raman Amplifiers cover both the C and L-Band ranges, providing a wider amplification range. The "Others" category includes Raman amplifiers that operate outside of these specified wavelength ranges, targeting niche applications or specific wavelength bands.</p></p>
