<p><p>Roll bending machines are used in various industries to bend and shape metal sheets and plates. The market for roll bending machines can be categorized into three types: 3-roller, 4-roller, and other. The 3-roller machines are equipped with three rolls, where two rolls work as pinch rolls and the third one provides the bending force. In 4-roller machines, the additional roll acts as a support to prevent slipping during the bending process. The "other" market category includes machines with different configurations or those that utilize different mechanisms to accomplish the bending task.</p></p>
