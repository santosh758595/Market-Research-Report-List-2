<p><p>The drywall suspension system market consists of two main types: hidden and external. </p><p>Hidden drywall suspension systems are designed to be concealed and provide a seamless finish to the ceiling. They typically consist of metal grids or channels that are installed above the ceiling and support the drywall panels while remaining hidden from view. This type of system offers a more modern and clean aesthetic appeal.</p><p>On the other hand, external drywall suspension systems are visible from below and add a decorative element to the ceiling. These systems are often made of metal or wood and can be customized to complement the design of the space. They are more commonly used in spaces where aesthetics are a priority, such as high-end residential properties or commercial buildings.</p></p>
