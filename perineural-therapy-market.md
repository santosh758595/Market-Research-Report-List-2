The market for perineural therapy is divided into two types: internal neurostimulation and external neurostimulation. </p><p>Internal neurostimulation involves the implantation of devices, such as electrodes or stimulators, directly into the body near the nerves to deliver electrical impulses for pain relief. </p><p>On the other hand, external neurostimulation involves the application of electrical currents to the skin's surface using devices like TENS (transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation) units. This method is non-invasive and offers temporary pain relief.</p></p>
