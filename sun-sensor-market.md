<p><strong>The Sun Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coarse Analog Sun Sensors</li><li>Fine Analog Sun Sensors</li><li>Digital Sun Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sun sensor market types can be classified into three categories: coarse analog sun sensors, fine analog sun sensors, and digital sun sensors. Coarse analog sun sensors provide a general indication of the sun's position using simple analog outputs. Fine analog sun sensors offer more precise measurements with higher resolution, enabling accurate determination of sun angles. Digital sun sensors utilize digital outputs to provide highly accurate and detailed measurements, often incorporating additional functionalities such as temperature compensation and communication capabilities. These market segments cater to different industries and applications based on the required level of accuracy and functionality.</p></p>
