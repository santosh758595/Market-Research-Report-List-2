Satellite communication utilizes satellites orbiting the Earth to enable long-distance wireless connectivity. LPWAN offers low-power, long-range communication suitable for IoT devices. Cellular M2M involves wireless communication between machines using cellular networks. All these types contribute to the Wireless Connectivity IC market by providing different wireless connectivity solutions for various applications.</p></p>
