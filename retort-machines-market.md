<p><p>Retort machines are used in the food industry to sterilize packaged products, ensuring their safety and prolonging shelf life. There are two main types of retort machines: batch sterilization and continuous sterilization. </p><p>Batch sterilization involves loading a specific quantity of packaged products into the retort chamber and subjecting them to high temperature and pressure for a fixed period. This type is suitable for small to medium-sized production batches.</p><p>On the other hand, continuous sterilization involves a constant flow of packaged products through the retort system, allowing for continuous processing. This type is ideal for large-scale production lines with high output demands.</p></p>
