<p><p>Shot blasting machines are widely used in various industries for metal surface preparation and cleaning. There are five main types of shot blasting machines in the market. Hanger type machines suspend the workpiece, allowing 360-degree shot blasting. Tumblast machines tumble the workpiece, ensuring complete coverage. Continuous through-feed machines enable continuous blasting of long products. Rotary table machines rotate the workpiece, optimizing coverage and efficiency. Other types include overhead rail machines, pipe machines, and wire mesh belt machines. Each type caters to specific applications and offers different features for efficient and effective shot blasting.</p></p>
