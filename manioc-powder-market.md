<p><p>Manioc powder, also known as cassava powder, comes in two market types: sweet and bitter. Sweet manioc powder is made from the sweeter variety of cassava root, containing less cyanide and a milder taste. It is commonly used in baking, cooking, and as a thickening agent in various recipes. On the other hand, bitter manioc powder is made from the more toxic variety of cassava root, which requires thorough processing to remove the harmful cyanide. Bitter manioc powder is mainly used in traditional dishes in some regions where extensive processing techniques are applied.</p></p>
