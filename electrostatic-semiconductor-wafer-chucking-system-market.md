<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Electrostatic Semiconductor Wafer Chucking System market consists of two main types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks utilize electrostatic attraction between the chuck and wafer to hold them together. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek Type Electrostatic Chucks use mechanical deformation of a soft elastomer layer to generate electrostatic forces. Both types are used in semiconductor wafer processes, allowing for secure and efficient wafer handling while ensuring precise positioning and minimizing damage during fabrication.</p></p>
