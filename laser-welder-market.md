<p><p>The laser welder market is divided into two main types: AC laser welder and DC laser welder. AC laser welders use alternating current to generate laser beams, making them suitable for welding materials with high electrical conductivity. On the other hand, DC laser welders utilize direct current, allowing for higher precision and control during the welding process. Both market segments cater to different welding needs, with AC laser welders being more suitable for specific materials and DC laser welders offering more precise welding capabilities.</p></p>
